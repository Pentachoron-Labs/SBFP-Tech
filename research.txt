
Contents of Ores
Monazite: helium (by heating), Thorium, trace uranium, lanthanum
Rutile: Titanium Oxide (TiO2), used as a pigment/extraction of metal if necessary
Cinnabar: Mercury. Toxic.
Limonite: Yellow iron ore, used for pigment and iron extraction
Arsenopyrite: Arsenic, iron, sulfur (add nitric acid), toxic and magnetic when heated
Fluorite: Hydrofluoric acid (toxic, duh), calcium
Molybdenite: Molybdenum(used in high-strength steels)
Pyrolusite: Manganese
